## How the 2025 competency maps changed from the 2022 competency maps

The updated competency maps were developed based on the following principle considerations:

To align with the rest of WOG, it adopts the "functional competencies" of the WoG/PSD and our People Development
competency frameworks.

* The new competencies and proficiency levels are based on the WoG's technology competencies and proficiency levels,
  adapted to fit our context.
* Selected critical and relevant competencies from the WoG/PSD's "Data and AI" job function.
* Added elaboration for our context and defined more competencies where relevant.

To keep the granularity of the competencies reasonable for technical assessment, the working group made a conscious
effort to limit the number of competencies to not more than eight (8).

* Retained the "Key Tasks and Performance Standards" to encompass other general competencies required for the job role.
* Tech Panel will assess both the "Key Tasks and Performance Standards" and the "Key Competencies".
