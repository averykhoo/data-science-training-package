### How to Use This Table

This table serves as the "constitution" for your competency framework.

1. **For Individual Contributors:** It provides a detailed roadmap for career growth.
   To move from PL1 to PL2, a data scientist can see they need to shift their focus
   from mastering *steps* (Procedural Knowledge) to mastering   *principles* (Conceptual Knowledge).
2. **For Managers & Mentors:** It gives them a precise, multi-faceted language for feedback. Instead of saying "you need
   to be more strategic," a manager can say, "You're operating at a strong PL2 by solving every problem we give you. To
   reach PL3, I want you to start focusing on the *evaluation* aspect: for your next project, bring me a comparison of
   three potential approaches and a recommendation, rather than just the final solution."
3. **For Assessors & Panelists:** It is the ultimate calibration tool. It trains them to look beyond the surface-level
   complexity of a presented solution. They can ask questions designed to probe a candidate's proficiency along each
   dimension:
    * **To test PL2 vs. PL3:** "Can you explain the underlying assumptions of the model you chose? What would happen if
      those were violated, and what alternative would you have considered then?"
    * **To test PL3 vs. PL4:** "The solution you built is excellent for this problem. If you had to design a framework
      so that ten other teams could solve *similar* problems without your help, what would that look like?"
